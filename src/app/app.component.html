Health information

COVID-19 affects people in different ways. Most infected people will develop a mild to moderate form of the disease and recover without hospitalization.
The most common symptoms:

fever;
cough;
fatigue;
loss of taste or smell.
Uncommon symptoms:
sore throat;
headaches;
headaches and muscle aches;
diarrhea;
skin irritation or discoloration of the fingers or toes;
red or irritated eyes.

In severe cases, symptoms such as:

severe lung infection,
sepsis and septic shock - generalized infection and inflammatory response,
all of which require specialized care and medical care.
The patient's condition can deteriorate rapidly, often in the second week of illness.

Recommendation

Get the Covid-19 Vaccine   

Keep physical distance of at least 1 metre from others, even if they don't appear to be sick.
Avoid crowds and close contact. Wear a properly fitted mask when physical distancing is not possible and in poorly ventilated settings.
Clean your hands frequently with alcohol-based hand rub or soap and water.
